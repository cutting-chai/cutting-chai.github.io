Introduction

While writing the book ‘Once Upon a Chai’, the author ended it with a riddle or rather a message which predicted the Dooms day & evil approaching.

Due to an uncertain disaster, the book burned down and reached hell, it came in the hands of the Angel of Death (Azrael) who then delivered the message to the gatekeeper of purgatory.

The message read, “The world is in danger. The protectors are dead. Revive the deadliest!”

After reading this, the gatekeeper grants an opportunity to those souls to purify themselves and do the good they couldn’t, by overcoming all the obstacles, levels and fighting to be the one in order to win the elixir of life and relive to accomplish the unfinished business.

The contingents would be dead antagonists who turned bad due to provoking reasons and who caused the bad for the good to take place.

A potion that grants the drinker eternal life.

Clause

The good doesn’t exist anymore, so the evil will take over & save the world! The innocent miscreants would be given a chance to escape purgatory and relive.

Who are we?

We are the gatekeepers of purgatory who give the contingents to do the good they couldn’t as their fate didn’t favor them & they were framed as villains.

--- layout: page title: Major Events excerpt: Top Upcoming Events of Cutting Chai ---

Dance

Features

  • Contingent has to Dance on dialogues. (1 minute minimum)
  • Usage of props. (bank)
  • Add a twist to the death of your villain.
  • Dance in the dark. (Pitch darkness on stage for minimum 30-45 seconds)

Instructions

  • Teams per contingent: 1
  • Number of participants: 7-10
  • Duration: 6-8 minutes
  • Eliminations

Drama

Features

  • One person playing 2 characters simultaneously.
  • Drama should be from the Point of view of the villain.
  • Mime. (1 minute)
  • Switch genres. (Include a plot twist that switches the genre of the play)

Instructions

  • Teams per contingent: 1
  • Number of participants: 5-10
  • Duration: 6-8 minutes
  • No mimicry, spoof and slang language
  • Eliminations

Band

Features

  • Acapella. (1 segment/song)
  • Original lyrics to an already existing composition. (In these lyrics, the participants must include “Why they deserve to win the chalice of life")
  • Mashup/cover/medley of greatest hits of a deceased musician. (bank)

Instructions

  • Teams per contingent: 1
  • Number of participants: 4-7
  • 20 mins with Setup
  • Eliminations

Fashion show

Features

  • Androgyny. (Participants must include androgynous clothes – blurring gender roles)
  • Indian twist to the attires.
  • Home decor as accessories/outfits. (Ex: Curtains can be used as a skirt, Blanket can be used as a shrug, Carpet can be used as a cape, etc.)

Instructions

  • Teams per contingent: 1
  • Number of participants: 8-10 (not including designer and the choreographer)
  • Duration: 5-7 mins
  • Designer, choreographer and showstoppers have to be registered
  • Eliminations

Investigative Journalism

The event calls for the participants to solve a case presented to them using various suspects, witnesses and clues around the campus.

The formal news report containing the solution to thecae must be submitted at the end of the events time limit.

Instructions

  • Teams per continent: 1
  • Number Of participants: 2
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Participants must carry laptops

Teleprompter

The event requires participants to read a news article from the teleprompter. Obstacles of all kinds are what the news readers deal with.

Instructions

  • Teams per continent: 2
  • Number Of participants: 2
  • Participants must complete the article

Damage Control

There’s trouble brewing and your character is in danger but worry not because there’s always hope. We shall give you a fair chance to justify the accusations on your client. Do you think your client deserves to be safe? Well then, prove it with a good point.

Instructions

  • Teams per continent: 1
  • Number Of participants: 1
  • Duration Round 1: 2+1 min (speech + answer)
  • Duration Round 2: 3 to 5 minutes
